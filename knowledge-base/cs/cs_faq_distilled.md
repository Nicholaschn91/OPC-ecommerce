## 物流与追踪
**Q**: When will my order arrive?
**A**: Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days. You can track your order here: [tracking link]. If there's a delay, we'll notify you by email.

**Q**: How do I track my package?
**A**: A tracking number is sent to your email when the order ships. Click the link or enter it on the carrier's website.

**Q**: Do you ship internationally?
**A**: Yes! International orders take 7-21 days depending on destination. Customs fees may apply.

**Q**: My item arrived damaged, what do I do?
**A**: I'm sorry about that! Please send photos of the damage to our support email within 48 hours. We'll send a replacement or refund promptly.

## 退换货
**Q**: What is your return policy?
**A**: We offer a 30-day return window. Items must be in original condition. Customized products cannot be returned unless defective.

**Q**: How do I get a refund?
**A**: Once we receive the returned item, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.

## 定制与个性化
**Q**: Can I add a name or photo to this product?
**A**: Absolutely! Leave your customization details in the order notes at checkout. Custom orders add 1-2 extra processing days.

## 产品材质与质量
**Q**: Is the print permanent or will it fade?
**A**: We use dye-sublimation printing — the ink bonds into the fibers. With proper care (cold wash, air dry), it won't fade or crack.

**Q**: What material is this made of?
**A**: This is made of premium [material]. See the full specs on the product page for detailed material composition.

**Q**: Can I wash this in the washing machine?
**A**: We recommend cold hand wash and lay flat to dry. Avoid bleach and tumble dry to maintain quality.

## 尺寸与适配
**Q**: What size should I choose?
**A**: Check the size chart in the product images. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up. Feel free to share your measurements and I'll help!

## 礼品与包装
**Q**: Do you offer gift wrapping?
**A**: We don't do traditional gift wrap, but we can include a handwritten gift note — just leave your message in the order notes!

**Q**: Will the price be visible in the package?
**A**: No! All packages are plain boxes/bags with no branding or price information. Perfect for gifts.

**Q**: What payment methods do you accept?
**A**: We accept credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

## 订单与支付
**Q**: Can I cancel or modify my order?
**A**: You can cancel within 1 hour of placing the order. After that, modifications depend on the production stage — contact us ASAP.
